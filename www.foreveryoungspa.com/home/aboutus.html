Meet Our Team

Kayla- Kayla is a certified Aesthetician/ skincare specialist who has worked in the spa industry for 11 years. Kayla graduated from the Advanced Aesthetics program at Gina's College of Advanced Aesthetics in 2009, where she fell in love with the beauty world. She now specializes in Dermalogica facials and manicures/pedicures, Kayla loves being the aesthetician her clients can go to for a pick me up or simply for self care, she looks forward to meeting you and helping you look and feel your best,

Britta-Britta is our glam queen, from editorial, wedding, natural or even education there is nothing this girl can’t do. Britta has been in the make up industry for 8 years, specializing in editorial, wedding and glam make up application. She has published with Playboy, Maxim, RHK, & Perfect Sports just to mention a few. Britta’s true passion is make up and make up education, her attention to detail shows in every look she does & we could not be more happy to have her on our team.

Sandra - Sandra is our Registered Reflexology Practitioner and is a current member in good standing of the Reflexology Registration Council of Ontario (RRCO). Sandra’s expertise in reflexology has been recognized by many. A Cambridge Reader’s Choice Diamond award winner in 2017, 2019, 2020 and again in 2021. Sandra also specializes in Reiki and Indian Head Massages.
